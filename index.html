About VH Print's

VH Print's is poised to become India's leading creator-driven gifting platform. We bridge the gap between talented designers and consumers seeking unique, personalized gifts. Our model empowers independent creators by providing them a low-barrier entry (₹49 joining fee) to showcase their designs on a variety of products like T-shirts, mugs, pens, custom notebooks, and more.

Our Vision: To cultivate a vibrant ecosystem where creativity flourishes, designers earn consistently, and consumers discover an unparalleled range of personalized gifting options. We handle the complexities – printing, high-quality production, nationwide delivery, and dedicated customer service – allowing creators to focus solely on their art.

The Opportunity: Bridging the Gap

The Problem

  • For Creators: High entry barriers, significant upfront costs for equipment & inventory, marketing challenges, and logistical nightmares (printing, shipping).
  • For Consumers: Limited availability of truly unique, personalized gifts. Mass-produced items lack a personal touch.
  • Market Gap: Difficulty for independent Indian designers to monetize their talent effectively at scale.

Our Solution: VH Print's

  • Empower Creators: A nominal ₹49 joining fee gives access to our platform. Upload designs, set your preferred items, and earn.
  • Hassle-Free Operations: We manage everything from printing on demand, quality control, secure packaging, to nationwide delivery and customer support.
  • Unique Gifting: Consumers get access to a vast, ever-growing catalog of unique designs from talented Indian artists, perfect for any occasion.

Our Sustainable Business Model

Creator Onboarding

Designers join for a one-time fee of ₹49, gaining immediate access to upload and monetize their designs.

Commission Structure

Creators earn attractive commissions ranging from 5.25% to 10.25% on the selling price of each item featuring their design.

Example: T-Shirt Sale

A T-shirt sold for ₹469. If the designer's commission rate for this product/tier is set, for example, at a point that earns them ₹51.31, this demonstrates a tangible earning per sale. Higher tiers yield more.

VH Print's Role

We manage the e-commerce platform, product sourcing, printing, quality checks, packaging, pan-India delivery, and all customer service.

This symbiotic model ensures profitability for VH Print's while providing a lucrative platform for designers and unique products for customers.
